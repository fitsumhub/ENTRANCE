Physics Course

Explore the world of physics from basic principles to advanced topics!

Course Overview

This course covers mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, optics, and modern physics. By understanding fundamental physics principles, you’ll be able to apply them to real-world scenarios and exams.

Topics Covered

  • Mechanics: Newton's laws, motion, energy, and momentum.
  • Waves and Sound: Properties of waves, sound waves, Doppler effect, and wave equations.
  • Optics: Reflection, refraction, lenses, and optical instruments.
  • Electromagnetism: Electric fields, magnetic fields, Ohm’s law, and electromagnetic induction.
  • Thermodynamics: Laws of thermodynamics, heat transfer, and efficiency.
  • Modern Physics: Quantum mechanics, relativity, and atomic models.
